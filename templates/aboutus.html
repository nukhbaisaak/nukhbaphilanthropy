OUR VISION

We believe in a world where everyone can realise their potential.

OUR MISSION

We are committed to improving the lives of vulnerable communities in low-income countries around the world, with a particular focus on women and children. With the right support and access to new opportunities and choices, we believe that those living in extreme poverty can actively shape their own destinies and begin to thrive.


OUR VALUES

Our work is defined by:

RESPECT

for the communities we engage with

TRUST

that shapes our relationship with our partner organisations

INTEGRITY

that drives our commitment to accountability and continuous improvement

OUR FOCUS AREAS

We provide support to non-profit organisations across three key areas :

ACCESS TO BASIC SERVICES

SUSTAINABLE LIVELIHOODS AND ECOSYSTEMS

EMERGENCY RESPONSE

HOW WE WORK
